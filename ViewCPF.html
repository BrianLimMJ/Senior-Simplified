CPF Balance Guide

Through this guide, you will learn how to view
your CPF account balance

Step 1: Launch the App

Step 2: Click on the finance icon to Log in

Step 3: Enter your CPF Pin

Step 4: Your CPF is hidden for privacy reasons

Step 5: Press the eye icon button on the top left to unhide your balance

Step 6: You can now view the balance of your CPF account
