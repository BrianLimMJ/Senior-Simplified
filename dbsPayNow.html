DBS PayNow Guide

Through this guide, you will learn
how to use PayNow to Mobile number

Step 1: Launch the App

Step 2: Click on the PayNow icon to Log in

Step 3: Enter your DBS UserID

Step 4: Enter your password

Step 5: Press the Log in button on the bottom

Step 6: Press "PayNow to Mobile"

Step 7: Select the number you want to PayNow

Step 8: Review and confirm the number you want to PayNow

Step 9: Press "Amt in SGD"

Step 10: Enter the amount you want to transfer

Step 11: Confirm amount to PayNow

Step 12: Review amount to PayNow & hit "Transfer Now"

Step 13: And with that you're done!
