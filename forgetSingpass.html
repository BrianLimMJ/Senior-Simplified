Reset Singpass Guide

Through this guide, you will learn how to reset
your Singpass account

Step 1

Step 1: Go to www.singpass.gov.sg

Step 2

Step 2: Press on the Log in button

Step 3

Step 3: Press the Use 'Password login instead'

Step 4

Step 4: Press the Reset password

Step 5

Step 5: Key in the necessary details

Step 6

Step 6: Press the Next button for CPF to generate a OTP

Step 7

Step 7: Key in the OTP sms that you have just received

Step 8

Step 8: Press the submit button

Step 9

Step 9: Key in your new Singpass password

Step 10

Step 10: Press the submit Button

Step 11

Step 11: There you have it! Your Singpass password has been reset, now you can go back to the Singpass App to Login
